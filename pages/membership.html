JOIN SEDS UNLV


Interested in joining SEDS UNLV? Members gain access to our projects, competitions and workshops! Develop your teamwork and leadership skills through technical projects, and gain invaluable hands-on engineering and science experience. Members also gain opportunities to attend conferences such as SpaceVision and expand their network to further their career. You can become a member of SEDS UNLV for an annual fee of $25.

To join, fill out and submit the following form and submit the $35 payment via PayPal to confirm your membership.

HANDS-ON EXPERIENCE

As a member of SEDS, you will be immersed in the design and development of rockets, payloads, and other space systems.

NETWORKING

SEDS members gain networking opportunities; connect with aerospace companies, professionals, and students across the country.

T-SHIRT

All SEDS UNLV members receive a T-Shirt!
